                                              Playstation 4, windows and Xbox One in Februrary 2019, for Ninetendo
                                              Switch i March 2021 and for Playstation 5 and Xbox Series X/S in March
                                              2022. A mobile version of the legends of the game designed for touchscreen
                                              titled Apex Legends Mobile was released in May 2022 on Android and IOS,
                                              The game supports cross-platform play, excluding the aforementioned mobile
                                              platforms.


                                              Before the match, players from into two-or-three-players squads and select
                                              from pre-designed characters with distinctive abilities, known as "legends".
                                              The game has two gameplay modes. In "Battle Royale", up to 20 three-person
                                              squads or 30 two-person duos land on an island and search for weapons and
                                              supplies before attempting to defeat all others players in combat. Appex
                                              Legends is set in the same science fiction universe as respawn Entertainment's
                                              Titanfall series, with several characters from the titanfall series appearing
