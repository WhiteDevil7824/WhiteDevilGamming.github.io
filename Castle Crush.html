                            About This Game



                                                                             Jump into the World of Csastle Crush, the incredibly popular from the team at
                                             Wildlife studios where players summon legendry monsters and sling powerful
                                             spells as they fight epic duels in magical arena! In this real-time-lane-based
                                             auto-battler,players have to design unique decks from thier collections of cards
                                             to form strategies and synergies to defeat thier enemies in fast-paced matches.


                                             At the core of the Castle Crush experience are the real-time PvP battles where
                                             players will have to take to the field of battle with the unique deck of cards they
                                             have build. Decks are made up of 14 minions and spells chosen from 68 umique
                                             cards that currently exist in game. Each card belong to one of four rarity tiers:
                                             Common,Rare,Epic and Legendary; players can put up to two copies of any card
                                             into their deck except for the legendaries, which are so powerfull only one of
                                             each is allowed at a time in any deck.
