                            About This Game



                                                                     Gangster Vegas is an open world action-adventure video game developed by gameloft
                                              Montreal and published by Gameloft. It was released for Android and IOS(iPhone, iPade
                                              Touch and iPad) on June 7, 2013. It is the sixth(seventh counting Gangstar city) installment
                                              in the Gangstar series; it is proceded bt Gangstar Rio: City of Saints and Succeeded by Gangster:
                                              New Orleans.


                                                                     The story revolves around Jason Malon, a skilled mixed martial arts fighter who is a
                                              fighter who is targeted by Frank Veliano after winning in a match. Like Games in the
                                              Grand Theft Auto series, Gangstar vegas has element of driving games and third-person
                                              shooters, and features "Open World" gameplay that gives the players more control over
                                              their playing experience.
