                                  About This Game



                                                                Clash Royale is a free-to-play real-time strategy vidio game developed and
                                                                published by supercell. The game combines elements from collectible card
                                                                games, tower defense and multiplayer online battle arena. The game was
                                                                released globally on March 2,2016. Clash Royale reached $1 billion in
                                                                revenue in less than a year on the market. In three years. Clash Royale
                                                                made $2.5 billion in revenue according to market intelligence company
                                                                sensor Tower.
