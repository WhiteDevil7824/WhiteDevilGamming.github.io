                            About This Game



                                                                     Mortal Kombat is an American media franchise centered on a series of video game
                                              origannaly developed by Midway Games in 1992. The development of the first game
                                              was origanally based on an idea that ED Boon and John Tobias had of making a video
                                              game starting Jean-claude Van Damne, but when that idea fell through, a science fantasy-
                                              themed fighting game was created instead. Still, the developers paid homage to him with
                                              Johnny Cage, a fictional film star whose personal style resembles Van Damme's. The
                                              original Mortal Kombat was the first fighting game to introduce a secret fighter,
                                              reached if the player fulfilled a set of requirements.
