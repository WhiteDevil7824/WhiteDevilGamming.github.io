                            About This Game



                                                                               Ludo King ia na Indian free-to-play mobile game application developed by
                                              Indian studio Gametion Technologies Pvt Ltd, based in Navi Mumbai, India.
                                              It is develped on the Unity Game engine and is available on Android, IOS,
                                              Kindle, Window Phone and Microsoft Window platforms. The game is a
                                              modernization of the board game, Ludo which in turn is based on the ancient
                                              Indian game of Pachisi.


                                                                               Ludo King was released on February 20, 2016, on the Apple Store, and has
                                              consistently rankes No. 1 in the Top Free Games Section of both the Apple
                                              App Store and Google App Store. It is the first Indian gamming app to pass
                                              500 million downloads. Ludo King is played 30 countries and available in
                                              14 languages.
