                            About This Game



                                                                    Into The Dead 2 is a first person shooter released for the Nintendo Switch
                                        by versus Evil. It was original released on mobile devices. The game is an
                                        arcade-style shooter where the player has to fight zombies and survive.
                                        The game includes additional mission based on Night of the living Dead
                                        and Ghostbusters.


                                                                    The player has to escape the zombie horde and shoot the ones encountered.
                                        The game includes 60 story levels plus challenges and 36 side stories across
                                        7 chapter. The player can use 25 unique weapons across 8 animal companions
                                        to help the player fight and level up with abilities.


                                                                    The all new sawblade launcher weapons and bear campanion give you
                                        the meaning power to keep you moving HD Rumble, Joy-con and Pro
                                        Controller support. Experience the Ghostbusters add-on and George A.
                                        Romero's Night of the living Dead Add-on content: each sold separatly,
                                        and included with your purchase of the eshop bundle or the cartridge.
