                            About This Game



                                                                     Lonewolf is the eponymous villain protagonist of the 2016
                                              Flash game Lonewolf-A Sniper Story. He is a former US
                                              soldier turned hitman who commits various murders for the
                                              criminal organization known as The Assembly in order to
                                              get close to its leader.
