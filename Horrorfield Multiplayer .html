                            About This Game



                                                                    Horrorfield is a freemium horror multiplayer mobile video game developed and
                                        published by Skytec Games. It was released on April 22, 2019 on both Android
                                        and IOS.


                                                                    According to game ranking aggregator whatoplay, Horrorfield has earned an
                                        aggregate ranking (playstore) of 8.12 for its IOS release and an 8.09 for its
                                        Android release.


                                                                    Horrorfield attempts to bring aspects of dead by Daylight into a mobile game,
                                        and be different. But, fails at the most crucial aspects of what qualifies as a
                                        horror game making the only thing players should be scared of is the fact
                                        they decided to even play this game.


                                                                    Horrorfield is a horrifying action horror game. Play this scary hide and seek
                                        online game with friend in real-time. Will you get caught by the deadly
                                        serial Killer or escape becomming a survivor? It's up to you in survival
                                        multiplayer games! Remember all horrer movies about cult manisc Jason
                                        and Friday the 13th and feel like the main character of a scary slasher.
                                        It's time to be afraid.
