                            About This Game



                                                                    Candy Crush Soda Saga is also known as Cabdy Crush 2, is a video game
                                        developed and released by King. It is a sequel to the legendry.


                                                                    In june 2014, a soft launch of Candy Crush Soda Saga was released, just
                                        for some countries. Four months later, the game was globally released on
                                        Facebook. Arround three weeks after the Facebook global release, it
                                        was officially released for all mobilies: Android and IOS.


                                                                    This game also appears on King.com since its first release data. However,
                                        as of March 31 2022, this game will no longer be playable on King.com
                                        website.


                                                                    Switch and match your way through new dimensions of gameplay in this
                                        juicy puzzel adventure. Embark on an epic journey through the magical
                                        Kingdom to discover the hidden secrets behind the Candy World. Take
                                        on this challenge alone or play with friends to see who can get the
                                        hieghest score! This game is completely free to play but some in
                                        game items such as extra moves or lives will require payment.
