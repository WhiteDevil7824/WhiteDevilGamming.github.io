                            About This Game



                                                                    Endless Nightamare is a 3D horror based puzzel and stand-alone game.
                                        The connected story and horror setting will hopefully deliver an unusual
                                        game experience for players.


                                                                    In this game you play as a police inspector James. your wife and daughter
                                        were murdered. In search of clues across various rooms of a mansion in
                                        order to find the murderer. The game has multiple endings, as you unlock
                                        them, the objective is to find the murder who killed your wife and daughter.
                                        Don't be fooled by this plain-loking mansion, as dangers lurk in every
                                        corner, so be careful as you look for clues in the dark. Your goal, first
                                        and foremost, is to survive...



                                                                    ● Investigate
                              ● Listen
                              ● Escape
                              ● Hide
                              ● Strategy


                                                                    Gameplay in Endless Nightmare is pretty simple. You move by using joystick
                                        on the left side of the screen. All you have to do is tap the screen to see the
                                        scenario from various perspectives. If you want to open a box or wardrobe,
                                        on the other hand, tap on the white indicators when they appear.


                                                                    Endless Nightmare has various levels of difficulty for you to choose from
                                        according to your skills level. No matter what, this game will have you on
                                        the edge of your seat, waiting for the next thing to make you jump. Parts
