                            About This Game



                                                                    Special Forces Group 2 is a first-person shooter game developed by Russian game
                                             developer ForgeGames and published by the GD Company. It was released for android
                                             in July 2016, and later it was released for IOS in October 2016, starting with version 1.5,
                                             as the successor to the original Special Forces Group. Currently, it has about 50,000,000
                                             downloads on Google play and has about 8,000 ratings on the App Store. It is succeeded
                                             by Special Forces Group announced in late 2021.



                                                                    There are currently nine game modes, which are available in both single-player and
                                             multiplayer modes:

                                                                    ● Classic
                                             ● Resurrection
                                             ● Capture The Flag
                                             ● Zombie Mode
                                             ● Bomb Mode
                                             ● Knives
                                             ● Deathmatch
                                             ● Arms Race
                                             ● Sniper
