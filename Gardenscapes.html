                            About This Game



                                                                    Gardenscapes is a casual match-3 game, released in 2016 for Android and IOS
                                        devices and also on Facebook.


                                                                    The original version of Gardenscapes, which was released for Microsoft Windows
                                        in October 2009, and was later released on OS X, IOS, Android, the Nintendo DS
                                        and 3DS, was a hidden objects game in which the player runs a B&B hotel. A
                                        sequel-Gardenscapes 2, was released in April 2013.


                                                                    According to VentureBeat, a mobile version was in development for over four
                                        years and was released in 2016 as a combination of match-3 gameplay with
                                        a city-building core. The storyline in centered on a butler named Austin, who
                                        strives to rebuild the dilapidated mansion and garden. To complete the
                                        chores, the user must complete levels and earn the required "stars".
                                        Gardenscapes allows players to choose decoration for cleaned-up
                                        areas of a massively overgrown garden as they level up. The core
                                        gameplay is completed with pin-pulling mini-games in which players
                                        need to save characters.
