                            About This Game



                                                                    Moredern Combat 5, also known as Mordern Combat 5: Blackout is a 2014
                                        first-person shooter developed by Gameloft Bucharest and published by
                                        Gameloft. It is the fifth installment of the Mordern Combat series and the
                                        sequel to Modern Combat 4: Zero Hour. It was released on July 24,2014
                                        for IOS, Android, Windows Phone 8, Windows 8.1 and BlackBerry 10, on
                                        October 24, 2018 for Windows via Stream, and for Nintendo Switch on
                                        February 14, 2019. It is the first game in the series that is developed by
                                        Gameloft Bucharest.


                                                                    In this the player can shoot, crouch, sprint, throw gerandes, aim, reload thier
                                        weapons, leap over obstacles, knife enemies, change/pick up weapons and
                                        use abilities supported by the equipped class. A new key feature to Mordern
                                        Combat 5 is the abillity to choose thhe soldier classes ranging from Assault,
                                        Heavy, Sniper, Recon, Support, Bounty Hunter, Sapper, X-1 Morph,
                                        Kommander and Marauder. Each of which has its own different perks
                                        and weapons. although a player's class does not preveent the player
                                        from picking up different classes weapons in-game. As the player
                                        continues in certain classes and acquires a "Weapon score", new
                                        guns and attachments to use in both thee campaign and multiplayer
                                        are unlocked. Mordern Combat 5 is the first Mordern Combat game
                                        in the series to use DRM and requires a constant internet connection
                                        to play.


                                                                    One major change in the campaign of Blackout, compared to previous
                                        Mordern Combat tuttles, is that the missions are shorter. A mission
                                        can last five to ten minutes, noticeably shorts than missions of previous
                                        entries. Mordern Combat 5: Blackout also introduces allies, characters
                                        who follow along with the player, often joining in during conflicts.
