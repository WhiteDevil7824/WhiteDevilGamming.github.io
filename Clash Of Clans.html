                                                About This Game



                                                                Clash Of Clans is a 2012 free-to-play mobile strategy video game
                                                                developed and published by Finnish game developer Supercell.
                                                                The game was released for IOS platform on August 2,2022, and on
                                                                Google Play for Android on October 7,2013.

                                                                The Game is set in a fantasy-themed persistent world where the
                                                                player is a chief of a village. Clash Of Clans tasks players to build
                                                                thier own village using the resources gained from attacking other
                                                                player's villages from troops; earning rewards, buying them with
                                                                medals or by producing them at thier own village. The main resources
                                                                are gold, elixir and dark elixir.

                                                                Clash Of Clans was released to generally positive reviews from critics.
