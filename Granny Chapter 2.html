                            About This Game



                                                                    Granny Chapter two is a first-person horror game and a sequel to granny,
                                        where you have to try to escape this evil grandma's home. And this time,
                                        Granny isn't alone. Armed with a bloody baseball bay, grandpa is also
                                        roaming this dwelling.


                                                                    Granny Chapter Two's controls are identical to the original game's. You
                                        can move your character freely through the settings using your left thumb,
                                        and your right thumb to look wherever you'd like. On the upper left-hand
                                        corner, you'll have access to the button to duck down and on the upper
                                        right-hand corner, you;ll find the action button.


                                                                    The goal og Granny: Chapter Two, just like in the first game, is to escape
                                        from the house before five days go by. Evry time the grandma or grandpa
                                        catch you walking through the house, They'll hit you and you'll lose an
                                        entire day. That's why you need to be sneaky and try to hide every time
                                        one of them sees you. It is a fun game, that will surely make you jump
                                        out of your seat on more than one occasion. Especially if you play with
                                        headphones as recommended.
