                            About This Game



                                                                     Free Fire Max is a battle royale game developed and published by gerena for Android
                                              and IOS. It become the most downloaded mobile game globally in 2019. As of 2021,
                                              Free Fire Max had surpassed 150 million daily active users. Free Fire Max, a graphically
                                              enhanced version of Free Fire, was released globally on 28 September 2021.


                                                                     Free Fire Max is a third-person shoter. The player can move their caharacter using a
                                              joystick and shoot and throw items with the fire button. In-game characters can jump,
                                              crawl and lie down. When on the battlefield, players can use a grenade called a "Gloo
                                              Wall" as a cover damage.


                                                                     Pets in Free Fire Max can assist players during combat. as of 2023, Free Fire Max has
                                              21 pets that player can take to battle. Each pets has a unique skills. While most pets
                                              skills are passive skills that trigger automatically there are some active skills that the
                                              player can activate manually. One of the pets in the game called poring was added to
                                              the game from collaboration with Ragnarok Online. Pets can be customized with skins
                                              and emotes.
