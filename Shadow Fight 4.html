                            About This Game



                                                                     Shadow Fight 4: Arena is one of fighting games. It is sequel of Shadoe Fight 3. This
                                              game was released only in cannada, and then was globally released on November
                                              3,2020. It is available in English, German, Italian, French, Chinese (Simplified and
                                              traditional), Spanish, Japanese, Portuguese, Thai, Russian, Turkish, Vietnamese and
                                              Indonesian. It is mainly about "player bersus player" gameplay. Each character and
                                              location in the game has its own unique connection with Shadow Fight 3's storyline.
                                              There are two modes of multiplayer in this game, Ranked and Unranked.


                                                                     There is a mode which offers the ability to fight with friends. Currently, it has two
                                              option Hosting and Joining. Each device will have to start their own fight code which
                                              can be sent to other player to invite them over and battle them. There is also a event
                                              mode that test the player's skills against other players with unique fight rules and
                                              refreshes evry week with new rewards and different fight rules. Plyers get to choose
                                              a randomized team of 3 characters with random perks of each character.
