                            About This Game



                                                                    Critical Oops is a multiplayer first-person shooter video game developed and published
                                             by Critical Force Ltd. It was released as Open Alpha in September 2015 for android and
                                             had been in the Open Beta until the official release in November 2018. Critical Oops is
                                             available on Google Play Store, Apple App Store and Amazon Appstore. It was available
                                             on PC through the Facebook Gameroom platform until july 10,2017.


                                                                   Critical Oops core gameplay is heavily influenced by the counter-strike series. NHN
                                             Entertainment Corporation and critical force distributed critical oops as now-defunct
                                             Critical Oops: reloaded in South Korea and in several countries of Asia.
