                            About This Game



                                                                    Dead trigger is a zombie themed first person survival horror video game
                                        developed and published by Madfinger game. It was developed in June
                                        2012 for IOS and Android mobile devices. The game is single player
                                        only. Author micah Nathan contributed to the script.


                                                                    The game starts at the map screen, where the players can select
                                        currently available missions or access in game features including
                                        a shop, casino and arena. There are always several generic
                                        missions available, as well as story missions.


                                                                    Dead trigger includes two form of currency: cash and gold is earned in
                                        missions, by dismembering zombies, collecting cash briefcases, and
                                        completing objectives. Gold can be earned in the daily bonus mission
                                        and the player is awarded a small amount of gold each time they level
                                        up.


                                                                    Cash and gold can be used to perchase weapons, equipment and
                                        character upgrades. Weapons are mostly based on real-life firearms
                                        such as the colt M1911A1 and AK-47. Dead Trigger offers pistols,
                                        rifles, shotguns, machine guns and melee weapons.


                                                                    All mission fall into several basic types, such as defending doors or
                                        killing a certain number of zombies. There are a limited number of
                                        location that are reused frequently in both story and random mission.
