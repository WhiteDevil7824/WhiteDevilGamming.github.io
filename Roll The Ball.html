                            About This Game



                                                                    Roll The Ball is an addictive puzzel game where you try to creat a track that takes
                                        ball from point A to point B. To do so there's a board full of defferent tracks that
                                        you'll have to drag arround to make the best possible track without any breaks
                                        along the way.


                                                                    There are 300 levels with several difficult modes: Beginner, Intermediate, Hard,
                                        Advanced and Extreme. Each mode has 60 insane puzzles that you'll have to
                                        try to solve in the fewest moves possible-or else risk getting fewest than all
                                        three stars.


                                                                    To move the tiles, use your fingurs to drag them wherever you want, joining
                                        them however you think best. You can only drag one tile at a time, Through,
                                        and you can't jump over a tile that's already there.


                                                                    If you're stuck and can't find a solution, you can always ask for a hint on how
                                        to pass the level as quickly as possible based on the moves you've already
                                        made. If you play daily, you'll get rewards that will give you a boost as you
                                        play.
