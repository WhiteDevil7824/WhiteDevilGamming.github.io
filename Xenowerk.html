                            About This Game



                                                                    Xenowerk is a top-down action shooter challenging your combat skills in
                                        an underground science lab. Descend deeper into the abyss and explore
                                        the tarnished levels. Armed with a flashing as your main guidance, you'll
                                        never know what's lurking in the dark !.


                                                                    Your objective is to destroy all the nests and eliminate all mutants to
                                        prevent them from spreading. Xenowerk has 50 levels to explore with
                                        increasing difficulty. As progresing through the game you'll come across
                                        a growing number of mutants and expanding areas to cleanse.


                                                                    Xenowerk features a set of a special power to boost ypur offensive when
                                        you're in nedd. Activate one or several at time for more effective way to
                                        wipe out mutants.
