                            About This Game



                                                                    Endless Nightmare: weird Hospital is a first-person horror games where
                                        you control a polic officer, James, who wakes up in an abandoned hospital
                                        full of horrors. Your goa; is to escape from this cursed place, trying to stop
                                        those responsible in the process. Your goal won't be easy at all.


                                                                    The controls in Endless Nightmare: Weird Hospital are very intuitive. you
                                        can move your character with your left thumb and move the camera with
                                        your right. On the right are also the buttons for aiming, shooting and
                                        reloading your weapons. To interact with any element in the setting,
                                        all you have to do is tap on it. By doing this, you can open doors, pick
                                        up objects from the floor and even kill any enemy you catch unawares.


                                                                    At the beginning of the game, your character doesn't have any special
                                        ability, but as you progress through the story, you can unlock new skills
                                        in a way similar to role-playing games. like this, the game incorporates
                                        an extra strategy element, as you have to decide which skills you want
                                        to focus on. Similarly, you can fashion new weapons and ammo out of
                                        the pieces you find around the settings.
