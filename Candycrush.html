                            About This Game



                                                                    Candy Crush Saga is a free-to-play tile-matching video game released by King on
                                        April 12, 2012, originally for Facebook; other version for IOS, Android, Windows
                                        Phone and Window 10 followed. It is a variation of their browser game Candy Crush.



                                                                    In the game, players complete levels by swapping colored pieces of candy on a game
                                        board to make a match of three or more of the same color, eliminating those candies
                                        from the board and replacing them with new ones, which could potentially create further
                                        matches. Matches of four or more candies create unique candies that act as power-ups
                                        with larger board-clearing abilities. Boards have various goals that must be completed
                                        within a fixed number of moves, such as collecting a specific number of a type of candy.
